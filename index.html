About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to nurturing an innovative mindset among children. We believe in education beyond the traditional rote learning methods and strive to equip the younger generation with the skills to address societal challenges through technology and innovation.

Our Activities

  • Developing educational content in Amharic for computer science disciplines like Scratch, Python, and web development.
  • Engaging children in science through innovative challenge projects to foster critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Providing a range of computer skills training from basic to advanced levels.
  • Guiding hands-on experiments and building of electronic devices and systems.
  • Offering mentorship to refine and improve children's innovative projects.
  • Inspiring with robotics programming and the creation of technological solutions.

Our Impact

Our impact is felt across various facets of educational and societal development:

  • Donation of over 200 books to bridge the resource gap for low-income students.
  • Facilitation of 40+ innovation projects by children to address and mitigate social issues.
  • Outreach to over 1,000 low-income students, providing them with quality education and skills.
  • Financial contributions totaling $1,185 to support educational resources and student projects.
